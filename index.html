What You Can Expect
    Properly editing and proofreading academic papers requires 15 to 20 minutes per page. Each sentence will be read three times: once for grammar, word choice, syntax, punctuation, and spelling; once for APA use; and once to gauge the flow between the previous and the subsequent sentence. Each source will be checked to verify it is in the references and vice versa.

    When working together on Path 1 or Path 2, we will develop your capstone project from the beginning as you move forward with your chair and committee regarding the content. We will set up monthly meetings to go over any progress and questions, and make sure to adhere to all APA and institutional requirements.
